PAYASAM

We do this all the time, but to use your breathing to find stillness, be more careful and conscious about it.
Pay attention to the rhythm. If you take short, quick breaths, try to move toward slower, deeper ones.
Put your hand on your belly: You should feel it rise and expand as you draw air in, and fall as you let it out.
Shoot for about six breaths a minute.
